This file contains information about the segmentation process for microglia (647 nm channel) of the REUS_VENAD_TREVC_Desiree project achieved via usage of the ImageJ macro 3_segment_MGs (general parameters as per version "3_segment_plaques_2024-09-23").
Three thresholds were chosen for normalization (TH1), exclusion of non-brain background (TH2) and inclusion of potential cell candidates (TH3), respectively.

Known potential issues to keep in mind during segmentation QC: Over detection in the striatum, under detection at brain edges
Known issues with know fixes: segmentation outside brain tissue/in ventricles (erodable after alignment), over detection in the cerebellum (cerebellum will be excluded from analysis).
